> at [Z] take rate. Construction in [market] slipped [N] weeks due to [cause];
> we expect to recover by [date]. Cash position at quarter end was $[A]M with

## 5. What's working

2–4 bullets. Specific. No adjectives. "Take rate in [market] reached X%
within Y months of homes passed, vs Z% expected" beats "great traction in
[market]."

## 6. What's not working

2–4 bullets. Same standard. This section is the one Greg actually reads
first. Be honest about it. If a thing is broken, name it, name the cause,
name the fix, name the date.

## 7. Strategic moves this quarter

Hires, departures, partnerships, market entries/exits, regulatory matters,
financing, M&A activity that's already public. One bullet each.

## 10. Risks we're watching

3–5 bullets. The risk, why we think it's elevated this quarter, and what
we're doing about it. If a risk listed last quarter has resolved, say so and
remove it next quarter.

## 11. Appendix — methodology / restatements

Any change in how a metric is calculated. Any restatement of a prior-quarter
